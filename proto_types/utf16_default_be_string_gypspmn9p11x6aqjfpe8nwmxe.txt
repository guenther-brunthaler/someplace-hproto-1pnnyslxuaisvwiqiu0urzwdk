"utf16_default_be_string" (UUID gypspmn9p11x6aqjfpe8nwmxe) - a UNICODE string in UTF-16 encoding without a particular normalization form (see "utf8_string"). A BOM may be used to indicate the byte order. Without a BOM, the byte order must be big-endian.
